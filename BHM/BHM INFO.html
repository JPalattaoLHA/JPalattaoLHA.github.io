Information

Juanita Westmoreland-Traoré became is the first appointed black judge in the history of Quebec.

She was born on March 10th,1942.

She was born in Verdun.

Her parents immigrated here from Guyana, in 1942.

She studied at Marianopolis, Universite de Montreal, and the University of Paris.

In 2012, she was reaching the mandatory retirement rate, so there was a conference held at the McGill Faculty of Law, to honor her.
